Jello

Description

This is a simple recipe for jello. It's not challenging, but it is delicious. Make it and learn why Jello is called the food of the gods.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of jello
  • 2 cups of boiling water
  • 2 cups of cold water
  • 1 cup of favorite fruit (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil 2 cups of water.
  2. In a bowl, mix the jello powder with the boiling water until dissolved.
  3. Add 2 cups of cold water to the mixture.
  4. If desired, add your favorite fruit to the mixture.
  5. Pour into a mold or serving dish and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
  6. Enjoy your yummy jello!
