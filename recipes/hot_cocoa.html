Hot Cocoa

This is a simple guide to making really yummy hot cocoa.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of milk to the dry ingredients and stir until you have a smooth paste.
  3. Gradually add the rest of the milk while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring frequently until it is hot but not boiling.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Pour into a mug and top with whipped cream or marshmallows if desired.
  7. Enjoy your delicious hot cocoa!
