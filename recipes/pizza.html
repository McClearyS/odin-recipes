Pizza

Description

This is a simple recipe for making a delicious pizza. Yum.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup of pizza sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your choice of toppings.
  7. Brush the edges of the crust with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and enjoy!
